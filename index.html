WELCOME TO happy tods

We are a Reggio Emilia inspired early learning center that seamlessly blends this philosophy with the best of the global curricular frameworks. Our approach ensures a strong foundation that is both internationally relevant and deeply meaningful in the early years.

At Happy Tods, we believe that the early years are not just foundational - they are transformational. Our unique play and discovery-based learning facilitated by world-class educators, in thoughtfully designed home-like spaces make up our approach to Early Years. We believe that Happy Childhoods Shape Lifetimes!

Who We Are

A Space for Happy Childhoods

Happy Tods is a loving, child-centric center in Perambur, Chennai.

We believe every child deserves a safe, engaging environment to explore their world. Our philosophy blends play-based learning with structured activities to nurture intellect and emotion equally.
